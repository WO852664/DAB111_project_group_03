About the Data

Source of the Data

The data used on this website comes from the Canada Immigration dataset for the year 2019. This project is part of the DAB111 course at St. Clair College, focusing on Python programming and data analytics.

Definitions of Each Variable

  • Overall Rank: The rank of the country based on the happiness score.
  • Country or Region: The name of the country or region.
  • GDP per Capita: The gross domestic product per capita, indicating the economic output per person.
  • Social Support: The level of social support in the country or region.
  • Healthy Life Expectancy: The average number of years a person is expected to live in good health.
  • Freedom to Make Life Choices: The level of freedom individuals have to make their own life decisions.
  • Generosity: The generosity of the country's population.
  • Perceptions of Corruption: The perceived level of corruption in the country or region.
